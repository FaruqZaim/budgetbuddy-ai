The Problem

Students often lose track of where their allowance goes.

💸

Impulse Spending

Students may spend too much on food, drinks, entertainment, and transport without realising it.

📉

Poor Tracking Habits

Many students do not record daily expenses, making it hard to understand spending patterns.

😵

Complicated Apps

Traditional budgeting apps can feel too detailed or time-consuming for students to use consistently.

Our Solution

A simple Telegram budgeting bot powered by AI.

BudgetBuddy AI lets students record expenses using simple Telegram messages. The system stores spending data in Google Sheets and uses Gemini AI to generate personalised budgeting summaries, saving tips, and risk levels.

How It Works

The process is simple and student-friendly.

Profile → Expense Entry → Google Sheets → AI Summary
