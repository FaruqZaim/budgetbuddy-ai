The Problem

Students often struggle to track spending and manage allowance.

💸

Impulse Spending

Students may overspend on food, drinks, entertainment, and transport without noticing.

📉

Poor Tracking

Many students do not record daily expenses, so they do not know where their money goes.

😵

Complicated Apps

Budgeting apps can feel too complex, causing students to stop using them.

Our Solution

A simple Telegram budgeting bot powered by Gemini AI.

BudgetBuddy AI allows students to register their profile, enter expenses, and request an AI budgeting summary through Telegram. The system uses n8n to connect Telegram, Google Sheets, and Gemini together.
