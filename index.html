Problem

Many students struggle to manage their allowance and daily expenses. Common problems include impulse spending, poor tracking habits, and limited financial knowledge. As a result, students may run out of money before the end of the month.

Our Solution

BudgetBuddy AI is a Telegram-based AI budgeting assistant that helps students record expenses, understand their spending patterns, and receive personalised saving advice.

How It Works

Telegram → n8n → Google Sheets → Gemini → Telegram

Students can use simple commands:

  • /start - Start the chatbot
  • PROFILE | Name | Weekly Budget | Savings Goal
  • EXPENSE | Category | Item | Amount | Notes
  • /summary - Get AI budgeting advice

Features

  • Easy Telegram chatbot interface
  • Fast expense recording
  • Automatic date and time tracking
  • Last 7 days spending summary
  • Personalised AI saving tips
  • Low, Medium, and High spending risk levels
