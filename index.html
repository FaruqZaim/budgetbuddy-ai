Problem

Many students struggle to track their spending and manage their allowance. They may spend too much on food, transport, drinks, entertainment, or school supplies without realising how quickly their money is used up.

Budgeting apps can also feel complicated, so students may stop using them after a short time.

Solution

BudgetBuddy AI is a Telegram budgeting bot that allows students to record expenses quickly through simple chat commands.

The bot connects Telegram, n8n, Google Sheets, and Gemini to generate personalised AI budgeting advice.

How It Works

Profile → Expense Entry → Google Sheets → AI Summary

  1. User creates a profile with weekly budget and savings goal.
  2. User records expenses through Telegram.
  3. Google Sheets stores and organises the spending data.
  4. Gemini generates a personalised budget summary.
  5. The AI summary is sent back to the user on Telegram.

Features

  • Simple Telegram chatbot interface
  • Fast expense entry
  • Automatic date and time recording
  • Last 7 days spending summary
  • Personalised AI saving tips
  • Low, Medium, and High risk level
  • Student-friendly financial advice

Target Audience

BudgetBuddy AI is designed for full-time polytechnic and university students aged 17 to 25.

These students usually receive a weekly or monthly allowance and need a simple way to track food, transport, entertainment, school supplies, and savings.
